Robert Raynor

Hi, I’m Robert. I’m a technical lead at EigenLabs, a blockchain startup which is working on targeting and blowing up any and all bottlenecks which prevent blockchains from delivering on their full promise.

Previously, I was a PhD student at the University of Washington, focused on a mixture of deep learning theory and data marketplace mechanisms. Before that, I was an applied physicist in the US Air Force, working with advanced optical systems such as high energy lasers, plenoptic cameras, and holographic wavefront sensors.
